Who we are

We are a waste management company committed to keeping our environment clean and safe for a better future. We recycle platic and glass waste and utilize organic waste to make fertilizer as well as making feeds for both animals and birds. We have been operational since 20/01/2024 and look forward to making positive change in the society.
